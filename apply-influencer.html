Influencer Application

Enter any social media accounts that you own. Our minimum requirement for an Instagram or YouTube micro-influencer is at least 10K followers/subscribers. For blogs and review sites, a minimum of 50K unique page views per month is required. Please do not enter any accounts that do not meet this threshold as they will not be accepted.
After your application is accepted, you will need to verify you own the account by adding a short verification code to the end of your social media bio. After verification you can remove the code. This helps us prevent fake and impostor accounts and maintain a high quality experience for all.
For blogs and review sites, we will need to verify your past three months of site traffic through Google Analytics or similar. We only need temporary read-only access to these analytics for one-time verification and you can revoke access afterwards.
