What is Plaidish?

Plaidish is an online platform where influencer marketing and affiliate marketing meet. Influencers can find products to promote, and earn money via affiliate links. Brands can offer up products for promotion, and can search for influencers with relevant audiences.

We provide the platform for discovery and communication, a complete affiliate tracking system, and tools to handle payments.

For Brands

Tired of trying to measure the ROI on your influencer campaigns? Unlike Pay-Per-Click ads, which are vulnerable to clickfarms and fake followers, the Pay-Per-Sale model virtually eliminates fraud and lets you easily see where to allocate your marketing dollars.

It's well known that influencer marketing works, thanks to audience trust. Plaidish helps you find the influencers with relevant and engaged audiences, and handles the entire link tracking system for you. Simply integrate our tracking pixel into your store site, find influencers to promote your products, and watch the sales come in.
